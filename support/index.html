Simplistic Calculator

A simple, elegant scientific calculator app built with Flutter

Welcome to Simplistic Calculator! This app provides all the mathematical tools you need for everyday calculations and advanced scientific operations.

Features

  • Basic arithmetic operations: Addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division
  • Scientific functions: Sine, cosine, tangent, square root, power
  • Arc trigonometric functions: Arcsine, arccosine, arctangent
  • Advanced operations: Absolute value, sign, ceiling, floor
  • Mathematical functions: Natural logarithm and exponential
  • Additional operations: Factorial and modulo
  • Calculation history: Review your past computations
  • Clean interface: Intuitive, user-friendly design

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations are performed locally on your device.

Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data is tracked or sent to external servers
  • No third-party analytics or advertising services are used
  • All calculation history is stored only on your device and is never transmitted
  • The app works completely offline
